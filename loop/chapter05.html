As humanity progresses within the simulation, they start asking the same existential questions that once plagued the witch doctors. The Watcher of the Intertwining of Fate

In a secluded corner of Vodum, a solitary watcher observes the intertwining of fate. This guardian, tasked with overseeing the confluence of the tangible and virtual realms, now faces a unique challenge. As reality and digital existence begin to merge, the watcher must navigate this new, complex landscape. They vigilantly monitor the dance of converging realities, ensuring that the delicate balance between order and chaos is maintained. Their role becomes crucial as they guard against the insidious chaos that threatens to seep through the digital realm's imperfections.

Guardian of Eternal Echoes

In Vodum's most ethereal realms, the venerable Guardian upholds the continuum of existence. Witnessing the birth and demise of ages, they stand as a sentinel against the encroaching sea of doubts. This Guardian, wise beyond measure, understands the delicate balance of being and non-being. Amidst the growing tumult of human questioning, they strive to maintain harmony, ensuring that the fabric of existence remains intact despite the relentless challenges it faces.
