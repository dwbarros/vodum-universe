Chapter 1: The Last Stand of Vodum

In a world drained of its natural resources, under a perpetual twilight sky, the remnants of a once-thriving civilization lie in despair. Here, in a hidden sanctuary, the ancient witch doctors of Vodum gather, facing a dire choice: to let the world perish or to transform it entirely.

Characters

These Witch Doctors have merged themselves with technology and earthly elements in a desperate attempt to preserve life. They have crafted a simulated reality, becoming the hidden architects of this new existence – our existence.

Each of them are unique with a full profile including:

  • Intelligence, Wisdom, Vodum and Interfacing, with values from 0 to 1000.
  • Mysticism and Technicism, with values from 1 to 5.
  • Mystic Skill and Tech Skill
  • Mastery and Connections.

Mastery: A combination of individual Mystic Skill and Tech Skill, resulting in a unique ability. Connection: Represents the synergy among Witch Doctors' Masteries from the same realm, giving rise to a symbiotic power.

There are 10 possible connections, as follows:

  • Nexus Resonance
  • Celestial Synthesis
  • Ethereal Codex
  • Celestial Codex
  • Ethereal Confluence
  • Tidal Luminescence
  • Ethereal Convergence
  • Nexus Omnia
  • Ethereal Code Weaver
  • Omni-Realm Confluence
