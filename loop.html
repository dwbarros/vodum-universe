What existed before the Big Bang?

Scientific theories have long suggested that there was nothing before this momentous event, but recent discoveries and theories are beginning to challenge this notion. Simultaneously, the intriguing possibility that we might be living in a simulated reality gains credence.

As we delve deeper into this idea, it becomes apparent that the level at which our reality might be simulated is still unknown and open to exploration. This interweaving of cosmic origins and the nature of reality forms the foundational essence of "Loop," a narrative that ventures into these profound and uncharted territories.

Chapter 1: The Last Stand of Vodum

In a world drained of its natural resources, under a perpetual twilight sky, the remnants of a once-thriving civilization lie in despair. Here, in a hidden sanctuary, the ancient witch doctors of Vodum gather, facing a dire choice: to let the world perish or to transform it entirely.

Chapter 2: The Covenant of Roots and Wires

Centuries of isolated evolution have transformed the witch doctors. Their flesh and bones have merged with the earth's elements and advanced technology, creating a unique symbiosis. Realizing the impending doom, they unite their ancient knowledge and advanced science to form a Covenant, planning to save what remains of the world through a simulated reality.

Chapter 3: Crafting the Simulation

In a ritual combining chants and complex algorithms, the witch doctors weave ancient magic with quantum computing. Each contributes a vital element to the simulation: mystical herbs, life-threading USB wires, and reality-altering VR glasses. Their efforts culminate in the birth of a new, simulated reality.

Chapter 4: The Woven Reality

Reality as we know it begins anew, but the witch doctors don't disappear; they become the hidden architects and overseers of this woven world. They continue to tweak and adjust the simulation, ensuring life persists, all the while haunted by questions about what preceded their own existence.

Chapter 5: Unraveling the Threads

As humanity progresses within the simulation, they start asking the same existential questions that once plagued the witch doctors. The matrix of cables in the deadwood forest backdrop begins to glitch, revealing the fragility of this woven reality and prompting a few brave souls to seek the truth about their world.

Chapter 6: The Convergence

The quest for truth leads to the sanctuary of the witch doctors, where realities collide. The witch doctors reveal the nature of the simulated world and their limitations in understanding the universe's origins. They propose a new Covenant – a fusion of the simulated and the real to create a harmonious, looping reality of being and non-being.

Epilogue: A New Reality Forms

As the new reality takes shape, everyone, and everything becomes a blend of organic and inorganic, past and future, real and simulated. Life continues in an ever-changing yet constant loop, embodying a cycle of existence and inquiry. In this new reality, the witch doctors find peace, knowing that life, in any form, endures.
